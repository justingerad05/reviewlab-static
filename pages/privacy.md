# Privacy Policy

ReviewLab respects visitor privacy and collects minimal data necessary to operate this website.

We may collect anonymous analytics data, referral sources, and on-site behavior to improve content quality.

Email addresses submitted through forms are never sold or shared.

Some pages may contain affiliate links. If a purchase occurs, ReviewLab may earn a commission at no additional cost to the reader.
